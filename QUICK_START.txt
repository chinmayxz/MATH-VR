📌 FASTEST WAY TO PLAY (3 SECONDS!)
─────────────────────────────────────────
1. Double-click: index.html
2. Click: "Start Playing!" button
3. Done! You're in VR!


🎮 CONTROLS
─────────────────────────────────────────
Desktop:
  • W/A/S/D = Move around
  • Mouse = Look around  
  • Left Click = Select answers

VR Headset:
  • Move head = Look around
  • Thumbstick = Walk
  • Trigger = Select answers


🌍 CHOOSE YOUR WORLD
─────────────────────────────────────────
In the game, click any environment:
  🏔️  Boulder Mountains (scenic & calm)
  🏝️  Hawaii Beach (tropical paradise)
  🌌  Outer Space (cosmic adventure)
  🌲  Forest (nature immersion)


🎯 GAMEPLAY
─────────────────────────────────────────
1. Click "Start Math Challenge"
2. Read the question in the floating 3D panel
3. Click on the correct answer box
4. Watch your score go up!
5. Build streaks for bonus points!


📊 SCORING
─────────────────────────────────────────
• Correct Answer = 10 points + streak bonus
• Streak Bonus = 2 points per consecutive correct
• Wrong Answer = Streak resets (try again!)

🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING
─────────────────────────────────────────
Problem: White screen?
Fix: Wait 5 seconds for loading, or refresh page

Problem: Can't move?
Fix: Click inside the game window first, then use WASD

Problem: Questions not loading?
Fix: No problem! Game auto-generates questions if API fails

Problem: VR button missing?
Fix: Use Chrome or Firefox, or try opening from VR headset browser
